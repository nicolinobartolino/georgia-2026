🚐 End of hike — how do we want to finish?

From Ushguli we have ~3 nights before the Sep 12 flight. Hard deadline: at KUT airport by 2:30 am. Both options work — it's a question of whether we want to add Tbilisi or not. All transfers by private car.

Option A — Kutaisi only simpler
D12 · Thu 10 Sep — Ushguli → Mestia by shared taxi (~1 hr), then Mestia → Kutaisi by private transfer (~4–5 hrs). Arrive early evening.
D13 · Fri 11 Sep — Kutaisi full day. Gelati, Bagrati, old town.
D14 · Sat 12 Sep — Airport by 2:30 am. Taxi from Kutaisi city ~15 min.
Option B — add Tbilisi recommended
D12 · Thu 10 Sep — Ushguli → Kutaisi by private transfer (~5 hrs). Break the journey, sleep in Kutaisi. Chance to see Gelati and Bagrati properly.
D13 · Fri 11 Sep — Kutaisi → Tbilisi by private transfer (~3 hrs). Arrive midday. Old town, Narikala, sulphur baths. Best vegan food of the whole trip.
D14 · Sat 12 Sep early am — Tbilisi → Kutaisi by private transfer (~3 hrs). Arrive Kutaisi, airport by 2:30 am for ~4:45 am departure.
⚑ Book all private transfers in advance. The Tbilisi → Kutaisi leg on Sep 12 is the critical one — confirm the driver the night before.
📋 Reference
🌱 Vegan — safe dishes everywhere
  • Lobio — bean stew
  • Pkhali — walnut & herb pâtés
  • Badrijani nigvzit — aubergine/walnut rolls
  • Ajapsandali — slow-cooked vegetable stew
  • Mchadi — cornbread
  • Mexican fries — potato wedges, Georgian spices
  • Tomato & cucumber salad

Watch: Lobiani & spinach breads may contain egg. Khachapuri has cheese. Carry trail snacks — options thin out west of Mestia. Contact each guesthouse before arrival with dietary needs.

💸 Budget benchmarks
  • Flights: ~£350/person return (Pegasus via IST)
  • Guesthouses: ~£19–22/person (70–80 GEL) incl. dinner & breakfast
  • Transfer Kutaisi → Etseri: ~£40–55 total (150–200 GEL)
  • Private taxi Tbilisi → Kutaisi: ~£20 total (75 GEL)
  • Ushguli → Mestia shared taxi: ~£8/person (30–40 GEL)
  • Private transfers (Mestia→Kutaisi, Kutaisi→Tbilisi, Tbilisi→Kutaisi): ~£30–50 each (110–185 GEL)

1 GEL ≈ £0.27. Verify before travel.
